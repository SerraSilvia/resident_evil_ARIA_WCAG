Recapitulation about the chosen book:
The Crystal Egg, H.G. Wells


The story talks about an old man, Mr Cave, who is the owner of an antiques shop and he owns a special item there, a crystal egg. 

One day two men want to purchase it but he don’t want to sell it anyway. That night, he hid the egg and started to peer inside it. He discovered a little world inside. 

After that, he exposed to his friend, Mr Wace, an assistant of a hospital of London, what was showing the egg and invited him to investigate.

They deduced that the planet inside was Mars and Mr Wace took notes about the investigation.

Days later, Mr Cave appeared dead and buried and the egg was sold by his own family. The egg got lost and the only thing who remained was Mr Wace notes.
